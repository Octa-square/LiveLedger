LiveLedger - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 25, 2025

Summary: LiveLedger stores your data locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal sales data with anyone.

1. Introduction

Welcome to LiveLedger ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password when you create an account
  • Company Information: Company name (and logo for Pro users)

2.2 Sales Data (Stored Locally)

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Platform selection (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, custom platforms)
  • Sales transactions and payment status
  • Order details and customer names
  • Products, inventory, and stock levels

You can back up this data using "Backup to Files" (saves a JSON file to your chosen location) or "Restore from Backup" to restore from a previous backup.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Purchase Information: Subscription status (through Apple StoreKit)
  • App Usage: Features used, crash reports (anonymized)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your support requests
  • To improve our app based on usage patterns
  • To send important updates about the app
  • To process your subscription through Apple's App Store
  • To provide and maintain our service

4. Data Storage and Security

Your sales data stays on your device. We use Apple's secure storage to keep your data locally. This means your business information never leaves your phone unless you choose to:

  • Export to CSV
  • Use Backup to Files (saves to your chosen location on device or iCloud)

Account information (email, password) is stored securely and encrypted.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For managing in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Apple App Store: For processing subscriptions and payments

We do not share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Portability: Backup your data to Files or export to CSV
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Export your sales data to CSV or JSON backup
  • Access: View all data associated with your account

7. Children's Privacy

LiveLedger is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
