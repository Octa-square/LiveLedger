Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an order?

Simply tap on any product in the Products grid. A popup will ask for the buyer's name, then the order is added instantly!

Where is my data stored?

All your sales data is stored locally on your device. We never upload your business data to our servers.

How do I export my orders?

Tap the "Export" button in the header to download your orders as a CSV file that you can open in Excel or Google Sheets.

What happens if I close the app during a live stream?

Don't worry! LiveLedger auto-saves your data. When you reopen the app, all your orders and products will be exactly as you left them.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Go to Settings and tap "Upgrade to Pro" or tap the Pro banner that appears when you're running low on free orders.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription?

Yes! You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your Apple ID settings in the App Store.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings → Language and select from 13 available languages.
