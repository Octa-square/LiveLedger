## 📱 About LiveLedger

LiveLedger is an iOS app designed for live stream sellers who need to capture orders fast. Tap a product, add a customer — done. No forms, no delays, no missed sales.

### Key Features

- ⚡ **Tap-to-Sell** — Add orders in under 2 seconds
- 📊 **Real-Time Dashboard** — See total sales, stock levels, and top sellers
- 🧾 **Print & Export** — Generate receipts and CSV reports
- 📦 **Inventory Management** — Color-coded stock alerts
- 🌍 **13 Languages** — Works in your language and currency
- 🎨 **Beautiful Themes** — Multiple themes to match your style
